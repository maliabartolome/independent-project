My Sweet Girl,

The way her warm heart shines through her eyes — this is the look of the genuine love I only aspire to share with the beings I live my life around.

Her love is pure. Her affection is beautiful. Her dependency is the blessing that gives me purpose.

This moment and moments like this are what nurture my heart. I am as dependent to her as she is to me.

⁣

And I believe that’s the beauty of our friendship.

The Swaying Palms,

Nature is quite humbling. Its raw beauty, while so pure is incredibly captivating.

So effortless. Yet, so much growth and so much time.

It’s a beautiful feeling to stand in the presence of something so remarkable.

My Sweet Sweet Edward,

Your love takes me to a place I’ve never been before. It takes me to a life long partnership where I feel safe and secure — warm and content.

You inspire me to be the best version of my existence with your effortless ethics of diligence and genuineness.

My heart has been whole from my own self-love but you’ve shown me how capable I am of expanding my heart and loving without limitations.

Your presence is what I crave. Your intelligence is beyond anything I’ve ever seen in a mind. Your laughter is the sound of my purpose.

The universe deemed me to be deserving of your love. This is what I will cherish forever and always.

Our Sweet Girls,

There’s something so beautiful about watching two worlds blend together.⁣

Bringing Nika to Portland has been a lengthy process where I waited until I believed it to be the right time to share a new life with her.⁣

This moment captured is just that. The new life I wanted to share with her.⁣

Introducing new friends and a new life style.

She’s 7. We have less time to spend with each other as the days pass by. But I want moments like this for her and well, selfishly for myself.⁣

These are beautiful moments. Watching her enjoy her day. Playing in the water. Chasing after her friends. Hiking through stunning cliffs.⁣

As if we were home. But only now, we are home.
